Some users are confused about their current plan health and recent usage.

## Request

- whether the user is "doing well" or "falling behind"
- what they should pay attention to

- Avoid adding complex interactions
- Prefer small UI changes over big redesigns

- make wording more encouraging / actionable

## Constraints
- Do not introduce backend logic
- Do not change authentication
